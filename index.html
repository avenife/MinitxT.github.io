Hey, I'm Minit

A developer who builds custom Android ROMs from AOSP source and a photographer who shoots everything on a smartphone — the same device I just flashed.

Currently maintaining manaus (Moto Edge 40 Neo) — working on device tree, kernel optimizations, and keeping it updated with latest security patches.

By evening I'm chasing golden hour light. By night I'm staring at build logs. Both feel the same kind of satisfying when they come together.

I do all of this — the ROM building, the photography, the late-night kernel debugging — purely for fun. No contracts, no deadlines. Just a person who loves making things work, look good, and feel alive. Code and cameras are my playgrounds.
